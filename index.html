What causes the greenhouse effect?

    Here are three of the top contributors to the greenhouse effect:

  • Fossil Fuels and CO₂ Emissions

    Burning coal, oil, and gas releases CO₂, causing global warming.

    Agriculture (CH₄)

    Livestock farming emits methane, a potent greenhouse gas.

    Deforestation (CO₂)

    Cutting forests releases CO₂, worsening the greenhouse effect.
